    Company Profile


    M/s Straight Line Food Products Private Limited was originally incorporated in Kolkata in 2009 under the Companies Act, 1956 vide certificate of incorporation issued by the Registrar of Companies, Kolkata, West Bengal. We are a food packaging company and are engaged in the business of production and distribution of more than 30 varieties of biscuits. Our Production Unit at Asansol was founded and promoted by Mr. Pankaj Agarwal

    M/s Sakambari Processors Private Limited was originally incorporated in Kolkata in 2011 under the Companies Act, 1956 vide certificate of incorporation issued by the Registrar of Companies, Kolkata, West Bengal. We are a food packaging company and are engaged in the business of production and distribution of more than 25 varieties of biscuits. Our Production Unit at Dhaniakhali was founded and promoted by Mr. Pankaj Agarwal & Mr. Surendra Sawaria.

    Our Company believes in Brand building by manufacturing quality and healthy products. We aim to relentlessly offer distinctive flavours to our progressive consumers. We strive to reach deeper into the hearts of our loyal consumers, establishing a mutually enriching relation with each one of them. We believe that by focusing on bringing unique products to consumers not only brings sustainable growth for the company but enriches the consumers experience and builds trust in our brand.

    Our Vision


    Our Vision is to become India’s preferred biscuit company in next 5 years by providing our customers, the highest quality products and of the best hygiene standards. We want to foster an internal environment of innovation, collaboration and trust.

    Our Mission


    With the power of highly skilled team, emphasis on quality, integrated systems and assurance of reliability, we strive to achieve greater level of expertise in the food industry.

    • Mission 1 – Commitment to work efficiently and to foster access to sustainability and grow more.
    • Mission 2 – Promote reuse, reduce & recycling.
    • Mission 3 – Continuous environmental improvement. Strong partnership with neighbors, business & community.

    Our Values

    • High Standards are way of life. We respond to challenges and pursue continuous improvement.
    • We empower our people to make decisions that improve their work and benefit customers and company.
    • We strive for excellence in our work, products and services.
    • We encourage creative thinking and intelligent risk taking.
